# How to Update This Website

This guide is written for a priest, parish administrator, or volunteer who does not need to work with code.

## Before you begin

Open the website address followed by `/admin`.

Example:

`https://www.your-parish-site.org/admin`

Sign in with the email address and password that were given to you. If you do not have a login, stop and ask Waylight Atlantic for help before trying to change anything.

## Most common updates

Most people will only need the first few items in the left-hand menu:

- `Weekly Newsletter PDF`
- `Mass Times, Contact and Safeguarding`
- `Homepage Welcome and Images`
- `Homepage News and Notices`
- `Gallery Page and Albums`

## Step 1: Log in

1. Open the `/admin` page.
2. Enter your email address and password.
3. Wait for the editing screen to load fully.

## Step 2: Choose the area you want to edit

The main sections are:

- `Weekly Newsletter PDF` for the weekly bulletin PDF
- `Mass Times, Contact and Safeguarding` for next Mass, Mass times, livestream links, office details, and safeguarding contacts
- `Homepage Welcome and Images` for the welcome text, top homepage photos, and homepage feature sections
- `Homepage News and Notices` for current notices and links
- `Gallery Page and Albums` for parish photo albums and gallery images
- `Parish Documents and PDFs` for other downloadable files
- `Footer and Parish Name` for the parish name and footer contact details
- `Other Page Text` for less common page wording updates
- `Legal Pages` for Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy, and Terms of Use text

## How to upload the weekly newsletter PDF

1. Open `Weekly Newsletter PDF`.
2. Click into the newsletter list.
3. Add a new item for the new week.
4. Type the `Newsletter title`.
5. Choose the `Newsletter date`.
6. Use `Upload newsletter PDF here` and choose the PDF from your computer.
7. If you want, add a short note in `Short note under the link`.
8. Leave `Web link instead of a PDF upload` empty unless support told you to use it.
9. Save and then Publish.
10. Refresh the website and check the News & Reflections page.

## How to update Mass times

1. Open `Mass Times, Contact and Safeguarding`.
2. Open `Next Mass box on homepage` if you want to change the highlighted Mass box near the top of the homepage.
3. Open `Homepage Mass times boxes` if you want to change the smaller Mass boxes on the homepage.
4. Open `Mass and Services page sections` if you want to change the main Mass schedule page.
5. If you need to change the livestream link, open the section called `Livestream`.
6. Save and publish.
7. Check both the homepage and the Mass & Services page after publishing.

## How to update contact details

1. Open `Mass Times, Contact and Safeguarding`.
2. Open `Contact details and office hours`.
3. Update the church address, parish office phone, parish office email, priest details, parking, or office hours.
4. Save and publish.

These details are reused in several places, including the footer.

## How to update safeguarding details

1. Open `Mass Times, Contact and Safeguarding`.
2. Open `Safeguarding contacts`.
3. Update the parish representatives, diocesan contacts, or safeguarding links.
4. Save and publish.

## How to change homepage images

1. Open `Homepage Welcome and Images`.
2. Open `Top welcome section`.
3. Find `Hero slides`.
4. Open the slide you want to change.
5. Use the `Photo` field to choose a new image.
6. Fill in `Short description of the photo`.
7. Save and Publish.

## How to add a homepage notice

1. Open `Homepage News and Notices`.
2. Add a new item to the list.
3. Fill in the `Title` and `Short summary`.
4. Add the `Link to open`.
5. Turn on `Show this on the homepage` if you want it to be more prominent.
6. Save and Publish.

## How to update the gallery or add a new album

1. Open `Gallery Page and Albums`.
2. Open `Gallery albums`.
3. To add a new album, click `Add item`.
4. Fill in the `Album title` and `Short album text`.
5. Open `Photographs in this album`.
6. Add one item for each photo.
7. Use the `Photo` field to upload each image from your computer.
8. Add a short description in `Short description of the photo`.
9. Save and Publish.
10. Refresh the Gallery page and check that the album and rotating photos look right.

## How to add another parish document

1. Open `Parish Documents and PDFs`.
2. Add the title and date.
3. Upload the file.
4. Only use the web link field if the file already lives on another website.
5. Add a short description if needed.
6. Save and publish.

## How to save and publish

1. Use `Save` while you are still working.
2. Use `Publish` when the update is ready to go live.
3. Refresh the public website and check the page you changed.

## What not to change unless support is needed

- Do not change any field marked `Internal name - leave this as it is`.
- Do not change legal wording unless it has been approved.
- Do not change layout sections just to hide content.
- Do not replace safeguarding information with informal wording.
- Do not change links unless you are sure where they should go.
